Until the philosophy which holds one race superior and another inferior is finally and permanently discredited and abandoned; that there are no longer first class and second class citizens of any nation; that until the colour of a man’s skin is of no more significance than colour of his eyes; that until the basic rights are guaranteed to all without regard to race; that until that day.

The dream of everlasting peace and world citizenship and the rule of international morality will remain but a fleeting illusion to be pursued but never attained.
